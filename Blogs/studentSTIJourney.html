Year 2: Specialization and Real-World Readiness

The second year allowed us to dive deeper. We tackled subjects such as:

  • Mobile App Development

  • Systems Analysis and Design

The highlight of the year was our Capstone Project, where we developed a real-world application for a local business. Our group created a Daily Time Record with Payroll System using C# and SQL Server. It was both challenging and rewarding to present our final system to our panel.
